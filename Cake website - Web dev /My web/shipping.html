Delivery

We create elegant, delectable celebration cakes by hand, which, by their very nature, are delicate and require
the utmost care. All of our baked goods are packaged carefully, and you are welcome to pick them up from
our Oxford store.

We offer delivery for a fee within a 50-mile radius of our shops in our own Cake Shop vans by our dedicated
drivers in order to ensure that your cake arrives safely. For our more elaborate custom cakes and wedding cakes,
we will travel further afield, including London; therefore, please contact us so that we can calculate the delivery fee.

As many of our cakes are too delicate to be couriered, we charge a delivery fee to cover the cost of fuel, van
maintenance, and driver labour.

We offer our customers the following delivery timings:

  • Morning delivery (Monday to Saturday 8:30am – 12:30pm)

  • Afternoon delivery (Monday to Saturday 12:30pm – 5:30pm)

    • Our Cake Shop delivery drivers do not deliver on Sundays and Public Holidays.

    For all wedding cake orders we ask you for an indicative time which we will aim to deliver your cake by but we
    reserve the right to contact the customer or the venue to agree to amend the time if necessary.

    We recognise that some people will prefer to have a simpler cake, so we have designed a select range of cost-effective,
    quality quick cakes.

    Many of our cakes (including quick cakes) can usually be available within one week, although the most detailed,
    impressive designs can take a minimum of 2 weeks to create.

    Please give yourself ample time to order a celebration cake. We offer all of the cakes displayed on our website,
    but we can also design a custom cake for your special occasion.

    Contact us to schedule a free consultation to discuss your completely customised product.

    How long does it take to process an order?

    Please allow 3-5 days for your order to be processed for shipping.

    Who do you use to ship products?

    The shipping times will depend on the chosen couriers. Your orders are shipped via Royal Mail. Royal Mail aims
    to deliver in 2 to 3 business days, including Saturdays. Not included in these days are our processing times.

    Where do you ship to?

    At the moment we are shipping to UK destinations only.

    How do I check the status of my order?

    You can check the status of your order using the tracking number that you will receive via email after your order
    has been shipped. You may also contact Partybakeries@cakes.com for an update.

    Need help?

    Contact us at Partybakeries@cakes.com or call +44 7845 091775 for questions related to shipping, refunds or returns.
