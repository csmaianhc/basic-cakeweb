about

    The Party Bakeries has a great reputation because each of its high-quality celebration
    cakes is painstakingly made by hand. Cake designers and decorators who
    are professionals will work with you to design and create a one-of-a-kind cake specifically for your occasion.  

    Since we first opened our doors in the dynamic city of Seoul in 2003,
    we have been featured in a variety of local, as well as national and international, publications.

    The 26th Party Bakeries Shop, which is located in the historic Chester Market, is a common stopping pointon many of
    the city's walking tours. Here, both locals and visitors can admire the cake artists' creations.

    Since our company's inception, we have partnered with only the most reputable
    cake manufacturers in the United Kingdom to bring you cakes made with the freshest and most
    delectable ingredients. Find out about ingredients and allergens.

    The Cake Shop has a wide variety of cake decorating accessories available for purchase, such as edible items,
    ribbons, sugarcraft materials, tools, and equipment.

    Please visit our homepage if you are looking for additional information
    or if you would like to place an online order for one of our exquisitely handcrafted
    celebration cakes. You can have a more in-depth conversation with our team of designers about your specific
    requirements by visiting one of our stores or contacting us via the details on our homepage.
    Both options are available to you.
